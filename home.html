What is 'The Unpaid Bills' ?

The Unpaid Bills is a digital platform that shines a light on the everyday chores and emotional labour done mostly by women that are often overlooked or taken for granted.

The project's goal is to highlight this hidden labour by printing personalised, receipt-style records, transforming invisible efforts into visible stories and encouraging users to think about, acknowledge, and question injustice in their own homes.

What can you do?

- Notice the everyday tasks emotional and physical that often go unseen.

- Ask the women in your life how much they do. Really listen.

- Share the load cooking, cleaning, planning, caregiving without being asked.

- Talk to your family, friends, and children. Awareness spreads through conversation.

- Use your voice, online and offline, to advocate for change.
